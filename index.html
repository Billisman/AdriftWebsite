Welcome To ShiftGrade Studio

We are a group of students passionate about creativity, technology, and game development. This project was created as part of our minor program, where we combined our skills and ideas to design an interactive and enjoyable gaming experience. We created this game to explore the world of game development and challenge ourselves to turn ideas into reality. Our aim was to build something entertaining, interactive, and enjoyable for players while learning valuable skills throughout the process. This game represents our hard work, innovation, and love for creating digital experiences.
